CAHNRS Town Hall 2019

A Closer Look: The 2019–2020 Academic Year

Faculty, staff, and students in the College of Agricultural, Human, and Natural Resource Sciences are invited to join WSU President Kirk Schulz as he discusses the opportunities—as well as the challenges—that face the University during the current academic year. A question and answer period will follow the presentation.
