Creating powerful change can be as simple as making small shifts—shifts in the way we see others, in the way we see ourselves. This kind of transformative exploration is at the heart of Washington State University’s award-winning Tidal Leadership program, a 16-week online certificate course that helps participants cultivate high-impact, values-based leadership skills.

Offered through WSU’s Center for Transformational Learning & Leadership and the WSU Global Campus, Tidal Leadership is designed for anyone interested in learning readily-applicable skills grounded in authenticity, personal awareness, and compassion.
